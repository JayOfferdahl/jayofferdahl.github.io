About

I'm Jay, a computer science undergraduate at the University of Kansas in Lawrence, KS, and in my fourth semester of study towards a four year bachelor's degree program at KU's School of Engineering. I'm interesting in learning more about space and deep sea exlporation, as well as mobile and web application development.

I've learned a lot over the past two years at KU and on my own. Currently, I'm in an Embedded Systems class which has several exciting & challenging projects. For one of the labs, I decided to create a Pong video game on the display of the Stellaris LM3S1968 Micro-Controller. I used a two axis joystick as input, and was able to create a one player version of pong which tracked several variables and game modes. I really enjoyed working on this project and I plan to add two player functionality in a future lab, as well as some basic animation and sound effects.

Outside of school I enjoy my fair share of videogames (World of Warcraft, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Smash Bros). I also enjoy seeing movies, working out, warm weather, hiking, and hanging out with friends and family.
